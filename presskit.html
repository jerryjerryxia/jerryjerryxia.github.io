Description

“A translucent polygon glimmered coldly in the vast dark.
You had been here before — though God knows how long ago.”

Short: You ended the world as you knew it — and she gave you an endless summer as your reward. Endless Summer Syndrome is a surreal psychological visual novel of branching time-loops, choices that bite, and a summer that resets the moment it shatters.

You ended the world as you knew it. As your reward, Wang Shuang — your arch-frenemy — sealed you inside an endless, sun-drowned summer that resets the moment it shatters.

To get out, you have to knock her back to reality by whatever means necessary. Every loop is a choice. Every choice has a cost. Some of them are fatal — and that may be exactly the point.

Features

  • Multiple endings

    Through illusion and madness, arrive at reconciliation, ruin, or an ending you were never meant to find.

  • Not every path is bright and shiny

    Every choice can spawn a whole new timeline, with death and ruin never far behind.

  • Fully localized

    Play in English, Simplified Chinese, or Traditional Chinese — interface, narration, and audio, localized end to end.

  • A dreamlike score

    Hazy, ethereal music drifts as the summer slowly comes undone.

  • Vivid surrealism

    Hand-painted art melts a perfect summer into something quietly wrong — bright false skies, a silver desert of bones, and a giant venomous jellyfish that holds the fate of the world.
